The Problem With Facebook-Only Marketing

You Don't Own Facebook

Facebook changes its algorithm constantly. Organic reach has dropped to under 5% for business pages. One policy change, one account flag, and your entire marketing disappears overnight.

You Can't Rank on Google

When someone searches "AC repair near me" or "HVAC company Dallas," Google shows websites—not Facebook pages. Without a website, you're invisible to the majority of people actively looking for your services.

It Doesn't Look Professional

A Facebook business page looks like... a Facebook page. When customers are comparing you to competitors who have professional websites, you look like a smaller, less established operation—even if you're not.

Limited Information Architecture

Facebook doesn't let you organize information the way customers need it. Service pages, financing options, service area details, emergency contact protocols—these need a real website structure.
